Welcome to the Wiki Loves Monuments map, where you can explore and learn about built heritage.

Wiki Loves Monuments is the world’s largest photo competition, where everyone is invited to share their photographs of monuments on Wikipedia. Enter the name of a city (like “Berlin”) to explore a map of over 1.2 million monuments around the world.

In order to participate in Wiki Loves Monuments 2019 please follow these steps:

  1. Create account on Wikimedia Commons.
  2. Log in using button in the top-right corner.
  3. Use search box to find places with monuments.
  4. Drag and drop files on monument page.

We believe that photography is an important part of preserving and sharing our heritage. Over the last eight years, 46 thousand volunteers have participated in Wiki Loves Monuments, to share data and photos of monuments around the world. You can use this page to explore a map of monuments, learn more about it from Wikipedia and Wikidata, and donate your photos to Wikimedia Commons.
